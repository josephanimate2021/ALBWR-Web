This tracker is using a structure from a spoiler log found after generating a randomized game in v0.4.1 beta of the z17 randomizer created by rickfay in order to help precicely keep track your progress. It is also assuming that you are playing the vanilla game of ALBW with Normal logic. If you are playing the ALBW Randomizer, please upload your spoiler log in order for this tracker to keep track of your progress in a more accurate manner.
You may choose to track your progress based off of your spoiler log's item metrics ( JSON Spoiler Logs Only & BETA FEATURE ) or layout after upload to keep tracking easier for some setting configurations.
